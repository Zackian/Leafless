About Leafless I/S

We create games with personality, craftsmanship, and creative vision.

Company

Who We Are

Leafless I/S was founded by two software developers with a shared passion for games and digital experiences. We met during our studies at Dania Academy in Grenaa, completing both the Computer Science (Datamatiker) program and a Professional Bachelor in Software Development. Our shared ambition: to create our own games from idea to finished product.

How Leafless Started

In 2017, we launched Leafless Entertainment during an internship program, where we developed our first mobile game, Colorz. The project guided us through a full development cycle — from concept to release, teaching us the importance of finishing what we start.

Becoming Leafless I/S

As we grew, we rebranded to Leafless I/S and shifted focus to more ambitious projects. Since 2019, we’ve been developing our platformer, Gribley’s Grudge. The game has evolved through multiple design directions, helping us refine our vision.

Meet the Founders

Steffen – Co-Founder & Software Developer

I focus mainly on programming and the technical side of our projects, making sure our ideas actually work. Besides Leafless I/S, I also work as an IT consultant.

Patrick – Co-Founder, Software Developer & Visual Designer

I split my time between programming and the visual side of our games. I enjoy shaping the look and feel of our projects — from characters to worlds — while making sure everything works smoothly under the hood.

Our Vision

At Leafless I/S, we aim to create games with personality, craftsmanship, and a clear creative direction. We believe in experimenting boldly, refining continuously, and delivering memorable experiences.
